Portgas D. Ace

Ace, also known as Portgas D. Ace, is a beloved character in *One Piece*. He is the adopted older brother of Luffy and Sabo, and the son of the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. Ace was the commander of Whitebeard’s 2nd division and possessed the Mera Mera no Mi, a Devil Fruit that allowed him to control fire. Brave, loyal, and strong-willed, Ace's journey was marked by his search for purpose and belonging. His tragic death during the Marineford War deeply impacted Luffy and remains one of the most emotional moments in the series.
